About Me

Engineer. Innovator. EV Enthusiast.

I'm Raju Kasulavada, a Battery Pack Design Engineer from Siddipet, Telangana. My passion lies at the intersection of mechanical engineering and electric vehicle technology — designing battery systems that power a cleaner, smarter future.

Currently working at Ola Electric Technologies in Koramangala, I contribute to cutting-edge EV platforms including the Ola S1 Gen 3 scooter and the Roadster X Plus — bringing together deep knowledge of cell chemistry, thermal management, and structural packaging.
